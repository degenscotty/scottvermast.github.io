MINISQUARDON

MiniSquadron is one of my all time favorite games during my childhood. It is one of the very few mobile games I ever really got into. So I decided I wanted to remake this game for my first ever "big" assignment during my studies at DAE.

We got a very simple SDL framework that had the most basic gameloop ever and they said:
"Make whatever you feel like making, but use what we learned in class!"

First of all, I had to get my hands on some assets of the game, because there was no way I was gonna recreate all those airplanes. To my surprise, I managed to extract everything I needed from the actual .apk on my phone. The next tricky part was to create a moving paralax.

The gif above displays what the player view looks like (red rectangle) but also shows what is happening behind the scenes. That is what I love about video games: what happens behind the scenes!
